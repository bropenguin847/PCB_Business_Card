## Purpose
To design a more artistic and creative business card for the less tech savvy crowd. More experimentation and exploration on my end, while trying a new purple color from JLCPCB.

## Characteristics
Size: 85mm x 55mm
Color: Purple
Layers: 2
Via covering: Untented
PCB Thickness: 1.2mm
Mark on PCB: Order Number(Specify Position)
Drill holes: Yes

## Design
Font:
KODE MONO BOLD (MEDIUM)
Chinese character: WDXL_Lubrifont_SC

Logo: Updated logo to V2
